Experience

After nearly ten years of working in IT support — first for Comcast, then for a local school system — it's time for a change.

During my time with the Jefferson County School system, I built and launched the county's first online job application on the Drupal platform. Then, I created a ticketing system for our helpdesk, also on Drupal.

I was also responsible for a bi-weekly newsletter from the technology department to the district's staff. I ran the newsletter on MailChimp and was responsible for all parts of it including creation of the HTML template and the writing. This evolved into Pixed.
